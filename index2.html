Diyah Widiyasari Profile

Profile

Hi, I'm Diyah Widiyasari. I am a graduate with a Master's degree in Electrical Engineering, specializing in Microelectronics from the Bandung Institute of Technology (ITB). My primary research during my studies centered on contactless monitoring using Radar technology, with a focus on signal processing. I have expertise in data visualization, filtering techniques, and algorithm implementation using Python, Matlab, and Verilog. Throughout the course of my research, I engaged with devices such as FPGA, System On Chip, and microcontrollers. Please kindly check my portfolios on GitHub.

Research Interests

  • Signal processing for contactless detection using FCMW Radar
  • Hardware Design
  • Preprocessing Radar for human activity recognition
